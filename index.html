About

Born from years of friendship, music discovery, and trading vinyl, Compas Discoteca is a collective born out of a shared passion for the art of sound. Made up of four seasoned DJs—Ricardo, Albert, Adrian, and Elias—each member brings a distinct style, creating a dynamic fusion that defies categorization. From soulful tropical rhythms and undiscovered Latin gems to funk, disco, hip-hop, alternative, R&B, and beyond, the group curates a kaleidoscope of sound that celebrates both the familiar and the unexpected.

At its heart, Compas Discoteca isn’t just about moving feet on the dance floor—it’s about creating an atmosphere where music discovery and good times reign supreme. Whether spinning vinyl or embracing modern mediums, their performances weave together a story of shared passion and musical exploration. With years of experience rocking private events, clubs, lounges, and more, they thrive in any setting, delivering vibes that resonate with those who appreciate music’s timeless ability to connect.

Welcome to Compas Discoteca: a cornucopia of funkadelia and soulful grooves, where the dance floor meets the joy of discovery.
